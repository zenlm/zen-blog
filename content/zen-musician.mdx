zen-musician turns lyrics and a genre prompt into sung audio. It is the stage-1 model of the YuE pipeline, the part that produces the coarse audio-token track from text. It is for people building music tooling who want the YuE line packaged inside the Zen catalog.

Stage 1 on its own does not produce a finished song. Full generation also needs the stage-2 refiner, which we do not redistribute. Get it from [m-a-p/YuE-s2-1B-general](https://huggingface.co/m-a-p/YuE-s2-1B-general).

## Lineage

Redistributed from [m-a-p/YuE-s1-7B-anneal-en-cot](https://huggingface.co/m-a-p/YuE-s1-7B-anneal-en-cot) (Apache-2.0), by the M-A-P team. The model and its training are theirs.

We changed nothing. All three safetensors shards and the tokenizer are byte-for-byte identical to upstream, with matching SHA-256. There is no Zen fine-tuning in this checkpoint, no identity training, and no abliteration. It is a license-clean redistribution and nothing more.

## Use it

The repo ships YuE's raw `tokenizer.model` and no `tokenizer_config.json`, so it loads through YuE's reference pipeline rather than `AutoTokenizer`:

```bash
hf download zenlm/zen-musician --local-dir ./zen-musician

# github.com/multimodal-art-projection/YuE
python infer.py \
  --stage1_model ./zen-musician \
  --stage2_model m-a-p/YuE-s2-1B-general \
  --genre_txt genre.txt \
  --lyrics_txt lyrics.txt
```

Weights: [huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-musician](https://huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-musician)
