The default Zen5 tier: image and text in, text out, in a Q4_K GGUF that fits a single 24 GB card. Two files ship here, a 21.7 GB main GGUF and a 0.9 GB `mmproj` vision projector. Load both or you get a text-only model. 522 pulls so far.

## Lineage

Repackaged from [Qwen/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B](https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3.6-35B-A3B) (apache-2.0, Alibaba Qwen), quantized from the abliterated [huihui-ai/Huihui-Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-abliterated](https://huggingface.co/huihui-ai/Huihui-Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-abliterated) variant. Not trained from scratch, and not a Hanzo fine-tune. Zen's contribution is the GGUF conversion and redistribution under the upstream license, with LICENSE and NOTICE retained.

## Use it

```sh
hf download zenlm/zen5-gguf --local-dir zen5-gguf
llama-mtmd-cli \
  -m zen5-gguf/Huihui-Qwen3.6-35B-A3B-abliterated-ggml-model-Q4_K.gguf \
  --mmproj zen5-gguf/mmproj-model-f16.gguf \
  --image photo.jpg -p "Describe this image."
```
