A GGUF build of the large Zen embedding model, quantized to Q4_K_M so the 8B weights fit comfortably on a workstation. It is for retrieval and RAG indexing where you want the bigger encoder locally and can trade some precision for memory.

## Lineage

Quantized from [Qwen/Qwen3-Embedding-8B](https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3-Embedding-8B) (Apache-2.0), by Alibaba's Qwen team. Format conversion to GGUF Q4_K_M is the only change we made: no Zen fine-tuning, no identity training, no abliteration. If it is too heavy, take the [zen-embedding-0.6B-GGUF](/blog/zen-embedding-0.6b-gguf/) build.

## Use it

```bash
hf download zenlm/zen-embedding-8B-GGUF zen-embedding-8B-Q4_K_M.gguf --local-dir .

llama-embedding -m zen-embedding-8B-Q4_K_M.gguf -p "search query"
```

Weights: [huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-embedding-8B-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-embedding-8B-GGUF)
