zen-world generates video from a text prompt. It is the text-to-video entry in the Zen catalog, for teams who want the Wan2.1 T2V weights with Zen packaging and complete attribution.

## Lineage

Redistributed from [Wan-AI/Wan2.1-T2V-14B](https://huggingface.co/Wan-AI/Wan2.1-T2V-14B) (Apache-2.0), by Alibaba's Wan team. The diffusion transformer, the VAE, and the training behind them are their work.

We changed nothing. All six diffusion shards, the Wan2.1 VAE, and the umt5-xxl text encoder are byte-for-byte identical to upstream, with matching SHA-256. There is no Zen fine-tuning in this checkpoint, no identity training, and no abliteration.

## Use it

The checkpoint keeps Wan's native layout: `Wan2.1_VAE.pth`, `models_t5_umt5-xxl-enc-bf16.pth`, and `google/umt5-xxl/` alongside the sharded DiT. That is not a diffusers pipeline root, so there is no `model_index.json` and `WanPipeline.from_pretrained` will not resolve it. Run it with Wan2.1's reference code:

```bash
hf download zenlm/zen-world --local-dir ./zen-world

# github.com/Wan-Video/Wan2.1
python generate.py \
  --task t2v-14B \
  --size 1280*720 \
  --ckpt_dir ./zen-world \
  --prompt "a paper airplane banking over a canyon at sunset"
```

Weights: [huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-world](https://huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-world)
