The Zen5 Pro tier is the sparse MoE that fits on one 128 GB machine: an M3/M4 Max or a DGX Spark. Upstream config gives 43 layers, 256 routed experts plus one shared, six active per token, and a 1M position limit.

The recommended build is the IQ2_XXS routed-MoE file at 86.7 GB, imatrix-calibrated, with attention projections, shared experts, and the LM head held at higher precision. A 97.6 GB mixed-quant and a 164.6 GB Q4 build suit machines with more headroom, plus a 3.8 GB MTP draft-head file for speculative decoding.

## Lineage

A quantized redistribution, not a Hanzo fine-tune. The weights are [deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Flash](https://huggingface.co/deepseek-ai/DeepSeek-V4-Flash) (MIT), quantized to GGUF from the abliterated variant by huihui-ai. That abliteration is theirs; we did the quantization and the packaging. There is no separate zen5-pro fine-tune, so the upstream repo above is the parent.
