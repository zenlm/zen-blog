A GGUF build of the mid-size Zen reranker, quantized to **Q4_K_M** in a single 2.72 GB file. This is the middle of the Zen reranker line and the most downloaded of the three, which tracks: at under 3 GB it co-resides with a generation model on one card instead of competing with it for memory. In a RAG stack the reranker and the generator are both on the critical path, so what usually decides the build is whether both fit at once.

## Lineage

Quantized from [Qwen/Qwen3-Reranker-4B](https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3-Reranker-4B) by Alibaba's Qwen team, under the **Apache 2.0** license. This is a format conversion, not a retrain. For full-precision safetensors, see [zenlm/zen-reranker-4B](https://huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-reranker-4B).

## Use it

```bash
llama-server --hf-repo zenlm/zen-reranker-4B-GGUF \
             --hf-file zen-reranker-4B-Q4_K_M.gguf \
             --reranking

curl http://localhost:8080/v1/rerank -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d '{
  "query": "how do I tune a rerank endpoint",
  "documents": ["reranking guide", "unrelated doc"]
}'
```
