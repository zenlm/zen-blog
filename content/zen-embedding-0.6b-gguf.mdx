A GGUF build of the small Zen embedding model, quantized to Q8_0 for CPU and mixed CPU/GPU retrieval. Reach for it when you want local embeddings without a GPU budget.

## Lineage

Quantized from [Qwen/Qwen3-Embedding-0.6B](https://huggingface.co/Qwen/Qwen3-Embedding-0.6B) (Apache-2.0), by Alibaba's Qwen team. Format conversion to GGUF Q8_0 is the only change we made: no Zen fine-tuning, no identity training, no abliteration. The larger sibling is [zen-embedding-8B-GGUF](/blog/zen-embedding-8b-gguf/).

## Use it

```bash
hf download zenlm/zen-embedding-0.6B-GGUF zen-embedding-0.6B-Q8_0.gguf --local-dir .

llama-embedding -m zen-embedding-0.6B-Q8_0.gguf -p "search query"
```

Weights: [huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-embedding-0.6B-GGUF](https://huggingface.co/zenlm/zen-embedding-0.6B-GGUF)
