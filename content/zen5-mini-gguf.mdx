The agentic tier of the Zen5 ladder. One Q4_K_M file, measured at 138.3 GB. Reach for it when the workload is tool calling, code, and multi-step reasoning rather than open-ended chat. 243 pulls so far.

## Lineage

Repackaged from [MiniMaxAI/MiniMax-M2.5](https://huggingface.co/MiniMaxAI/MiniMax-M2.5), quantized from the abliterated variant by [huihui-ai](https://huggingface.co/huihui-ai). Not trained from scratch, and not a Hanzo fine-tune. Zen's contribution is the GGUF conversion and redistribution.

Note the license on this one: MiniMax-M2.5 ships under a [modified MIT license](https://github.com/MiniMax-AI/MiniMax-M2.5/blob/main/LICENSE), not plain MIT and not Apache-2.0. Those upstream terms carry through to this repo. Read them before you ship it in a product.
